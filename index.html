What is SUBI

SUBI comes from pagsusubi, a deep Filipino term meaning “to save” or mag-impok — the practice of setting aside money for the future.

SUBI PH is designed to help you monitor your cash flow in a simple, organized manner—empowering you to build financial discipline and make wiser decisions with your hard-earned money.
